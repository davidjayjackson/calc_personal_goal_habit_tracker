# Personal Goal & Habit Tracker

A goal-setting and daily habit tracking template for LibreOffice Calc. Define what matters, build consistent routines, review your progress weekly, and visualise an entire year of habit data at a glance.

## Sheets

| # | Sheet | Purpose |
|---|-------|---------|
| 1 | **Goals Overview** | Up to 10 goals with category, priority, target date, and progress |
| 2 | **Daily Habits** | Monthly habit grid — rows are habits, columns are days |
| 3 | **Weekly Review** | Structured reflection prompts for each week |
| 4 | **Milestones** | Log of key achievements with dates and notes |
| 5 | **Year at a Glance** | Heatmap showing habit consistency across all 52 weeks |

## Screenshots

### Goals Overview
![Goals Overview](images/screenshot-01-goals-overview.png)

Track up to 10 goals across categories (Health, Finance, Learning, Career, Personal). Each goal has a priority, target date, success metric, completion percentage, and status. Rows are colour-coded: green for On Track, amber for At Risk, blue for Achieved, grey for Paused. Includes a pie chart of goals by category and a bar chart of completion percentages.

### Daily Habits
![Daily Habits](images/screenshot-02-daily-habits.png)

Log 3–10 daily habits against every day of the month. Mark a cell `1` for done, leave blank or `0` for missed. Green cells = completed, red = missed. A completion percentage is calculated per habit, and a daily score row totals how many habits were hit each day. Includes a bar chart of habit completion rates for the month.

### Weekly Review
![Weekly Review](images/screenshot-03-weekly-review.png)

A structured reflection template with five prompts per week: what went well, what was challenging, which habits were maintained, which were missed and why, and the focus for the coming week. Also includes a 1–5 momentum score for each goal, with a line chart tracking momentum across all weeks.

### Milestones
![Milestones](images/screenshot-04-milestones.png)

Log significant achievements as they happen — the date, what the milestone was, which goal it supports, and how you felt. Over time this becomes a motivating record of how far you've come.

### Year at a Glance
![Year at a Glance](images/screenshot-05-year-at-a-glance.png)

A full-year heatmap with 52 columns (weeks) and one row per habit. Darker green = more consistent; light/white = low consistency; grey = weeks not yet reached. Provides a bird's-eye view of your habit patterns across the whole year, plus a bar chart of average days completed per week year-to-date.

## Getting Started

1. Open `personal-goal-habit-tracker.ots` in LibreOffice Calc — it opens as a new untitled document, leaving the template intact.
2. Save immediately as `.ods` with a meaningful name (e.g. `habits-2026.ods`).
3. Fill in your goals in **Goals Overview**.
4. Add 3–7 habits to **Daily Habits** (the habit names in column A).
5. Each day, mark completed habits with `1`.
6. Every Sunday, spend 10 minutes on **Weekly Review**.
7. Log achievements in **Milestones** as they happen.
8. Check **Year at a Glance** monthly to spot patterns.

---

## Tips

- **Start with 3–5 habits.** Adding too many at once leads to overwhelm. Build momentum first.
- **Mark habits at the same time each day** — consistency in tracking builds the habit of tracking itself.
- **Be honest in your weekly review.** The insights only help if they're accurate.
- **Revisit Goals Overview at the start of each month.** Priorities shift; update statuses and target dates as needed.

## Design Conventions

| Style | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Light blue cell | Input cell — enter your data here |
| White cell | Formula cell — do not overwrite |
| Green background | Completed / On Track / positive result |
| Red background | Missed / At Risk / over budget |
| Amber background | Warning — review needed |
